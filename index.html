Hi, I'm Liang Cheng and I am a visiting scholar at the PRECISE Center, University of Pennsylvania.

I have been working with Professors Insup Lee and Oleg Sokolsky of PRECISE center since Jan 2014. I am also an Associate Professor at TCA Lab, Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS). Before I had a position in ISCAS, I received my Ph.D in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China in 2009 under the supervision of Prof.Dengguo Feng and B.E. degree in Communication Engineering in 2003 from the same university.

My research interests focus on the security analysis and assessment of IT systemsin and medical cyber-physical systems, especially security issues in access control mechanisms and code implementations of these systems. Drop me a line if you're interested in working with me.
